# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Writers and visual-novel creators who need to draft scenes quickly, then add runtime behavior such as backgrounds, characters, choices, audio, and transitions.

## Product Purpose

Visual Novel Engine is a cross-platform editor and reader for building playable visual novels from canonical scene records and timeline steps. Success means a beginner can write a simple story fast, while advanced controls remain available without interrupting writing.

## Brand Personality

Focused, calm, practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered timeline-first editors for beginner writing, decorative dashboards, emoji-led controls, and permanent technical panels that compete with scene text.

## Design Principles

- Story text is the primary task.
- Scenes behave like ordered document pages.
- Technical actions are progressively disclosed through compact chips, panels, and slash commands.
- Mobile editing keeps the active writing position visible above the keyboard.
- Existing runtime power stays reachable without dominating the writing flow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable type, explicit button labels or accessibility labels, sufficient contrast, keyboard-aware mobile layouts, and reduced visual noise for long writing sessions.
